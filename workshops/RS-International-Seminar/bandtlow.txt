Title: Approximation of invariant measures for chaotic dynamical systems

Speaker: Oscar Bandtlow

Abstract: Invariant measures are important objects in the study of the
long-term statistical behaviour of chaotic dynamical systems. There are
very few cases where invariant measures with good statistical properties
are known explicitly. As a result there is interest in robust approximation
techniques. In this talk, I will give a survey of a number of methods and
will discuss their merits and shortcomings.
