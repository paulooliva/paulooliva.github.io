Title: Squares
Source: Spanish Site #00201
Classification: Modelling/Simulation - Representation Strategies
Problem Notes: The concept behind the solution is very straightfoward.
Technical Notes: The key to this problem is in how to represent the lines and squares and perform the necessary counting. Time spent in coming up with a good representation almost always results in less time coding the final product.
Solution: One method is to create a box structure with all four sides, and then a n by n matrix of such structures. Fill in the necessary lines, and the count squares using for loops.
