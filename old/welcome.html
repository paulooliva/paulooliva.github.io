Curriculum Vitae

Here is a short summary of my academic career: In 1994 I received a technical degree in Electronics at the Federal Technical School of Pernambuco, Brazil. At the end of 1998 I finished my undergraduate studies in Computer Science at the Federal University of Pernambuco, Brazil. In the summer of 2003 I concluded my PhD in Computer Science at BRICS, Denmark. In the beginning of 2004 I moved to London, to work at Queen Mary, University of London. I am currently employed as a University Research Fellow of the Royal Society and Reader of Mathematical Logic at Queen Mary University of London. For more details check my CV.

Research Interests

Logic: mainly classic, intuitionistic, and linear logic
Proof Theory: mainly proof translations and interpretations
Mathematical Logic: mainly reverse mathematics and computable analysis
